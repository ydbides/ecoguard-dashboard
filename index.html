About EcoGuard

EcoGuard Intelligent Aquatic Monitoring System

EcoGuard is a smart, cloud-connected aquatic monitoring system running on a Raspberry Pi 5. It combines real-time environmental sensing, Firebase cloud connectivity, dashboard analytics, and fish-specific intelligence to reduce human error and improve aquarium stability.

Sensors

EcoGuard monitors temperature, water level, turbidity, and pH in real time. The pH sensor measures water acidity/alkalinity on a 0–14 scale — a critical parameter for fish health that is now tracked and graphed continuously.

Core Features

Live monitoring, health scoring (including pH), pH strip visualisation and graph, sensor status, fish profile comparison with pH matching, tank suitability, analytics graphs, AI interpretation, browser alerts, and account-based preference saving.

Why It Matters

EcoGuard is built to help prevent fish deaths, reduce delayed reaction time, and make aquarium care smarter and more reliable — with pH now integrated as a first-class water quality metric.
