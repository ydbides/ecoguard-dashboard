EcoGuard Intelligent Aquatic Monitoring System

EcoGuard is a smart, cloud-connected aquatic monitoring and automation system designed to maintain optimal water conditions in real time. It combines environmental sensing, cloud connectivity, dashboard analytics, and fish-specific intelligence to reduce human error and improve aquarium stability.

Problem Statement

Aquariums can become unstable very quickly. Small changes in temperature, water level, or water clarity can stress fish, damage equipment, or lead to sudden losses. EcoGuard helps detect these conditions early and makes the system easier to monitor from anywhere.

Core Features

EcoGuard includes live monitoring, health scoring, sensor status indicators, fish profile comparison, tank suitability logic, analytics graphs, AI interpretation, browser alerts, cloud mode control, account-based preference saving, and manual output control for heater, pump, and light.

Why It Matters

The goal of EcoGuard is not just to display numbers. It is built to help prevent fish deaths, reduce delayed reaction time, and make aquarium care smarter, clearer, and more reliable.
